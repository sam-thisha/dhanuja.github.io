About

Priyanthi Athukorala has practiced criminal law exclusively since her call to the Kochchikade Bar in 1997. She is a senior member of the criminal defence bar with over 25 years experience.

As a trial lawyer, Ms. Athukorala has represented clients on a wide variety of charges. She has been counsel on hundreds of trials ranging from first-degree murder to drug trafficking to sexual assault. Ms. Page is passionate about protecting individual rights and freedoms. She works directly with each client to build a strong working relationship, reviews all options, and tirelessly prepares every case to provide the best defence.

She has been recognized in the media for fighting against police brutality and for the human rights of prison inmates. Ms. Page believes everyone deserves the best defence. To that end, she has represented clients both with and without financial means. Ms. Page is also an adjunct professor at the University of Colombo law school, teaching Trial Advocacy. She has taught criminal law at the Police Foundations program at Humber College.
